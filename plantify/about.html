About Us

Since started in Stockholm in 2016 by Steffe Stickling and Petronella, just Petronella, Plantify has revolutionized the way you think about plants in an office environment.

Plantify isn't just a delivery service for plants, it's a delivery service for new ideas.

Plantify makes it easy to keep your workplace fresh and oxygenated. A workplace that is alive and create ideas that live on.
